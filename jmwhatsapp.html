👻 Ghost Mode & Privacy Suite

01
Ghost Mode
Freeze your last seen, browse chats invisibly, and view statuses without anyone knowing you were there.
02
Hide Online Status
Stay invisible to specific contacts or everyone — appear offline while fully using the app.
03
Hide Typing & Recording
Type or record voice notes without the tell-tale "typing..." or "recording audio" indicator showing.
