🎯 Discovery & Gamification

01
AI Group Summarizer
Instant keyword-based AI summary of any group's recent activity, top topics, and community vibe score.
02
Weekly Missions
5 fresh challenges every Monday — join groups, write reviews, invite friends, browse categories — for bonus XP.
03
Group Tournaments
Monthly competitions between groups in the same category. Winner earns a 🏆 Champion badge for a full month.
04
Group of the Week Vote
Community-wide voting every week — the winner receives a 🏅 Community Choice badge announced every Monday.
05
Verified Creator Profiles
Level 5+ users who own groups can apply for a 🪪 Verified Creator badge shown across their public profile.
06
10-Tier Rank System
Progress from 🌱 Newcomer through Explorer, Scout, Veteran, Elite, Champion, Legend, to 👑 Grandmaster.

🔧 Group Management Tools

07
Invite Link Rotator
Auto-checks every group's invite link every 4 hours. Broken links trigger an instant admin DM with fix instructions.
08
Group Request Board
Post what group you're looking for — admins browse and claim requests, you get a DM with the match.
09
Group Mergers
Two small groups in the same category can merge — members redirect automatically, both admin teams notified.
10
Group Cloning
Copy a group's category, language tag, and welcome message to your own group in a single command.
11
Featured Listings & Boost
Feature a group for N days at the top of its category, or temporarily boost visibility for up to 48 hours.
12
Ownership Transfer
Formally transfer a group's GroupFinder ownership to another user, confirmed via DM before completion.

📢 Communication & Engagement

13
Weekly Admin Reports
Every Monday, group admins get a personalized DM: members, health score, rating, top topics, and tips.
14
Multi-Language Discovery
Browse groups filtered by language — English, Spanish, French, Arabic, Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Swahili, Chinese.
15
Anonymous Group Polls
Admins create anonymous polls — GroupFinder DMs every member privately and shows instant results.
16
Recurring Announcements
Schedule announcements that auto-repeat daily, weekly, or on specific days — perfect for rules reminders.
17
Welcome Message Builder
Custom welcome DM for every new member with a {name} placeholder for personalization.
18
Group Newsletters
Users subscribe to specific groups — admins write and broadcast newsletters directly to subscriber DMs.

🛡️ Safety & Intelligence

19
Duplicate Group Detector
Automatically flags similarly-named groups to admins when the bot joins, preventing fragmented communities.
20
Live Activity Indicator
Real-time activity dot on every card: 🟢 <15min · 🟡 <1hr · 🔵 <1 day · 🔴 older.
21
Auto-Tag Extraction
Analyzes messages every 2 hours and auto-extracts top discussion topics as hashtags on the group card.
22
Scam Link Scanner
Passively scans all messages for known scam and phishing patterns — auto-flags groups and alerts admins instantly.
23
Report & Appeal System
Report any group with a specific reason — scam, spam, inactive, wrong category. Auto-flagged at 5+ reports.
24
Member Count Verification
Verifies each group's real Telegram member count every 6 hours — no more stale, inflated numbers.
